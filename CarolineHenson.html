Pysgmy Marmosets consist of two different species of monkeys! They are known for being the smallest monkeys in the world. They have a very specialized diet of tree gum, where they gnaw holes into tree bark to hopefully let the sap pool up. But, they do supplement their diet with insects, fruit, and nectar. They love to stay in groups, and their groups are typically anywhere from 2 to 9 members, with 1 to 2 adult males and 1 to 2 adult females.
